## üöÄ How to Use

### In the Post Editor

1. **Open any post editor** (Write new post or Edit existing)
2. **Switch to the "Images" tab** in the right sidebar
3. **Upload or paste your image**:
   - Paste: `Ctrl+V` anywhere while editing
   - Upload: Click the upload area or drag & drop
4. **Review SEO data** (auto-generated but editable)
5. **Click "Insert"** to add to your content
